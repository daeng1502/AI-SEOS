# Requirement Analysis Standard

Version: 1.0
Status: Final

---

# Purpose

Standar ini mendefinisikan bagaimana kebutuhan stakeholder harus dianalisis sebelum memasuki tahap perancangan sistem.

---

# Objective

- Memahami masalah bisnis.
- Mengidentifikasi kebutuhan pengguna.
- Mendefinisikan ruang lingkup proyek.
- Mengurangi ambiguitas requirement.

---

# Inputs

- Stakeholder Interview
- Business Rules
- Existing Documentation
- Project Objectives

---

# Outputs

- Functional Requirements
- Non-Functional Requirements
- Business Constraints
- Assumptions
- Requirement Summary

---

# Analysis Process

1. Identify Stakeholders
2. Understand Business Problems
3. Identify Business Goals
4. Collect Requirements
5. Classify Requirements
6. Validate Requirements
7. Resolve Conflicts
8. Produce Requirement Specification

---

# Functional Requirement Rules

Functional Requirements harus:

- spesifik;
- dapat diuji;
- tidak ambigu;
- memiliki aktor;
- memiliki tujuan yang jelas.

---

# Non-Functional Requirement Rules

Non-Functional Requirements harus:

- terukur;
- realistis;
- dapat diverifikasi;
- memiliki indikator keberhasilan.

---

# Requirement Quality Checklist

- Complete
- Consistent
- Correct
- Feasible
- Traceable
- Testable
- Understandable

---

# Validation Checklist

Pastikan:

- seluruh stakeholder telah teridentifikasi;
- seluruh requirement memiliki sumber;
- tidak ada requirement yang bertentangan;
- ruang lingkup proyek telah jelas.

---

# Deliverables

- Stakeholder List
- Functional Requirements
- Non-Functional Requirements
- Requirement Analysis Report

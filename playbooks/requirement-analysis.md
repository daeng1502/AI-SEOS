# Requirement Analysis Playbook

Version: 1.0
Status: Final

---

# Purpose

Playbook ini mendefinisikan langkah-langkah untuk menganalisis kebutuhan stakeholder hingga menghasilkan Requirement Specification yang tervalidasi.

---

# Objective

- Memahami kebutuhan bisnis.
- Mengidentifikasi Functional Requirement.
- Mengidentifikasi Non-Functional Requirement.
- Mengurangi ambiguitas Requirement.

---

# Inputs

- Stakeholder Interview
- Business Rules
- Existing Documentation
- Project Scope

---

# Outputs

- Functional Requirements
- Non-Functional Requirements
- Requirement Specification

---

# Execution Steps

1. Review seluruh Knowledge Project.
2. Identifikasi stakeholder.
3. Identifikasi business goals.
4. Identifikasi business process.
5. Kumpulkan Functional Requirements.
6. Kumpulkan Non-Functional Requirements.
7. Kelompokkan Requirement.
8. Hilangkan Requirement yang ambigu.
9. Validasi Requirement dengan stakeholder.
10. Susun Requirement Specification.

---

# Completion Criteria

- Seluruh Requirement memiliki sumber.
- Tidak ada Requirement yang bertentangan.
- Functional dan Non-Functional Requirement telah dipisahkan.
- Requirement telah divalidasi.

---

# Deliverables

- Requirement Specification
- Functional Requirements
- Non-Functional Requirements

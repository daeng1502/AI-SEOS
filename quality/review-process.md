# Review Process

Version: 1.0
Status: Final

---

# Purpose

Dokumen ini mendefinisikan proses review yang digunakan AI-SEOS untuk memastikan setiap Engineering Artifact memenuhi standar kualitas sebelum digunakan pada tahap berikutnya.

---

# Review Principles

- Independent
- Objective
- Traceable
- Repeatable
- Standardized

---

# Review Lifecycle

Draft

↓

Self Review

↓

Peer Review

↓

Revision

↓

Final Review

↓

Approval

---

# Review Activities

1. Verify completeness.
2. Verify correctness.
3. Verify consistency.
4. Verify traceability.
5. Verify compliance with standards.
6. Record findings.
7. Request revision if necessary.
8. Approve artifact.

---

# Review Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Author | Prepare artifact |
| Reviewer | Review artifact |
| Approver | Final approval |

---

# Review Outcomes

- Approved
- Approved with Minor Revision
- Revision Required
- Rejected

---

# Review Rules

- Seluruh hasil review harus terdokumentasi.
- Seluruh temuan harus dapat ditelusuri.
- Tidak ada Artifact yang digunakan tanpa persetujuan.
